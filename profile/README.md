# MANARA Lab — UM6P College of Computing
**Multimodal AI, Networks, Analytics, Recommender Systems & Augmented Sensing Laboratory**  
University Mohammed VI Polytechnic (UM6P) · College of Computing · Benguerir, Morocco

---

## 🔭 About the Lab
MANARA Lab investigates the design and evaluation of **trustworthy, intelligent, and networked
systems** at the intersection of AI, cybersecurity, speech processing, and healthcare. Our
research spans algorithmic foundations and real-world deployable systems, bridging AI methods
with networked and physical system constraints. We place a strong emphasis on open and
reproducible science.

**Principal Investigator:** [Prof. Youssef Iraqi](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=0LdsuWgAAAAJ&hl=en)  
Senior Member IEEE · ACM Member · Associate Professor, College of Computing, UM6P

---

## 🧭 Research Axes

| Axis | Topics |
|------|--------|
| 🤖 Trustworthy & Explainable AI | Explainable AI (XAI), trust models, fairness, cross-domain and blockchain-based recommender systems, authorship verification |
| 📡 Wireless Networks & 6G | NOMA, IRS, UAV communications, HARQ, LEO satellite, IoT backhauling |
| 🔐 Security, Privacy & Blockchain | Physical layer security, cloud forensics, decentralized trust, federated learning with privacy guarantees, smart contracts |
| 🎙️ Speech & Affective Computing | Speaker identification, speech emotion recognition, overlapping speech detection, multimodal audio-visual processing |
| 🏥 Healthcare AI & Medical Imaging | Cardiovascular risk prediction, medical image analysis, AI-driven clinical decision support, student dropout detection |
| 📱 Mobile Crowdsensing | Task allocation, location privacy, crowdsensing frameworks |
